## New Features

### Pro subscription version upgrade

You can now upgrade all databases in your Pro subscription from your [subscription page](). 

Select **Version upgrade** to request to upgrade your subscription and databases if there is a later version available.



Select the version to upgrade your databases from the list and select **Upgrade** to submit the upgrade request.



The upgrade will start one week from your request, according to your subscription's [maintenance windows](). 

Review the [7.2 breaking changes]() or [7.4 breaking changes]() before you request to upgrade.
